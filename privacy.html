Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

At WebNexus, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or purchase our services/templates.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and billing details when you purchase a template or hire us.
  • Usage Data: Information on how you interact with our website, such as IP address, browser type, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Process transactions and deliver digital products.
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries.
  • Send updates, newsletters, or promotional offers (you can opt-out anytime).
  • Improve our website functionality and user experience.

3. Data Protection

We implement security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance your experience. You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect some site features.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@webnexus.com.
