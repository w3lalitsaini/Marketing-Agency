Refund Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Thank you for shopping at WebNexus. We strive to provide high-quality templates and services. Please read our policy carefully before making a purchase.

1. Digital Products (Templates)

Due to the nature of digital goods, all sales are final. Once a template has been downloaded, we cannot offer a refund or exchange.

However, we offer full support if you encounter:

  • Technical issues with the file download.
  • Major bugs or errors in the code that prevent typical usage.
  • Discrepancies significantly different from the product demo/description.

2. Service Projects

For custom web development and design services:

  • Initial Deposit: The 50% upfront deposit is non-refundable once work has commenced.
  • Cancellations: If you cancel the project midway, you may be billed for the hours worked up to that point.
  • Satisfaction Guarantee: We work closely with you through revisions to ensure you are happy with the final result before the final payment is requested.

3. How to Request Support

If you have any issues with your purchase, please email us at support@webnexus.com with your order details. We aim to resolve all issues within 24-48 hours.
