Refund Policy

Last updated: February 17, 2026

Thank you for shopping at WebNexus. We appreciate the fact that you like to buy the cool stuff we build. We also want to make sure you have a rewarding experience while you’re exploring, evaluating, and purchasing our products.

1. Digital Products

Since our templates and UI kits are digital goods delivered via Internet download, we generally do not offer refunds once the product has been downloaded. However, we understand that exceptional circumstances can take place.

2. Refund Eligibility

We will consider a refund request under the following circumstances:

  • Product Not As Described: If the template is materially different from the preview.
  • Technical Issues: If you encounter a bug that prevents you from using the template and we are unable to fix it within 3 business days.

3. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Purchases made more than 14 days ago.
  • "Change of mind" requests.
  • If you simply don’t have the technical skills to use the product (please check requirements before buying).

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please email us at support@webnexus.com with your order details and a description of the issue. We review requests on a case-by-case basis.
